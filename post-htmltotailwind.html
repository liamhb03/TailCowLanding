Best CSS/HTML to Tailwind Converters 2023

Liam HB

Liam HB

July 7, 2023

Trying to switch to Tailwind? Found an HTML element that would look great on your Tailwind site? HTML to Tailwind converters offer a quick way to bring your favorite designs to Tailwind. Here are our favorite converters.


LembaDev

LembdaDev is a collection of free developer tools. Pros: Free. Cons: Only convert CSS, not entire elements. Not a browser extension.


TailCow Convert

TailCow Convert is a browser extension that can transform regular HTML and CSS into beautiful Tailwind elements. Pros: Can convert whole websites and comes as a browser extension. Cons: One time purchase required.


Windy

Windy is a tool that makes it easier for developers to convert website elements to Tailwind. Pros: Copy directly from website using browser extension. Cons: Comparatively expensive.
